# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Eidolon is for operators, founders, and technical teams who run autonomous AI companies and need to supervise agents, tasks, approvals, budgets, knowledge, integrations, runtime sessions, and local/cloud execution from one control plane.

## Product Purpose

Eidolon exists to make AI agents manageable as durable company infrastructure. Success means an operator can define goals, hire and configure agents, inspect work, approve risky actions, connect tools, resume or cancel runtime sessions, and understand what the system is doing without leaving the dashboard.

## Brand Personality

Operational, technical, controlled. The interface should feel like a serious command center for autonomous work: dense enough for repeated use, clear under pressure, and explicit about runtime state.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-first landing pages inside the authenticated app, decorative AI chat shells, vague assistant magic, oversized hero sections, toy-like controls, and interfaces that hide failures behind optimistic status text.

## Design Principles

- Put operator state first: surface what is running, blocked, waiting for approval, or unsafe to continue.
- Prefer real controls over explanations: actions should wake agents, trigger routines, manage sessions, and reveal audit trails.
- Keep AI-company and personal-assistant modes connected: Jarvis surfaces should route into tasks, agents, approvals, skills, routines, and workspaces.
- Make local/cloud runtime differences visible: locality, capabilities, permissions, and health should be easy to compare before dispatch.
- Fail explicitly: unavailable runtimes, blocked tools, missing permissions, and stale sessions need visible states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessible controls. Preserve reduced-motion support for transitions, avoid color-only status indicators, and keep dense operational layouts readable on both desktop and mobile.
